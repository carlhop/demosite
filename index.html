College Life

My college days originally started at Alabama A&M University. Upon applying I viewed different majoors they had listed and looked for "Computer Engineering". Funny story about this is my goal going into college was to fix on the hardware of the computers. Going into the college process completely blind I chose Computer Science unaware that this meant I'll be working with the software of the computer instead of the hardware of the computer. After a few weeks I finally approached my instructor and told her what I really wanted to major in an what my plans were. She stated that Computer Engineering fell under Electrical Engineering. Once she informed me of that she also warned of the curriculum. The next day I went to look at the curriculum for Electrical Engineering and let's say I decided I'd be better off sticking with Computer Science. Eventually I transferred to Alabbama State University were I lost major credits and was dropped from a senior to a sophomore, but graduated May 7th, 2021. There were major differences between the two institutions. While A&M was more tech than State, the teachers at State taught fluently compared to those at A&M.
